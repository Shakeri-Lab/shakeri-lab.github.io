**If you are a UVA student:**
UVA undergraduate and graduate students are encouraged to reach out through email at hs9hd@virginia.edu. Our group is constantly in search of skilled UVA students eager to contribute to our diverse research endeavors. When reaching out, please attach your CV or resume, accompanied by a brief note detailing your research interests and objectives.

**Non UVA applicant:**
Thank you for your interest in our research group. Please note that Prof. Shakeri does not process Master’s or Ph.D. application submissions directly. We recommend submitting your application through the UVA official admissions website. The University of Virginia utilizes a comprehensive selection process to choose exceptional students from around the world. Upon your official admission, we can then explore opportunities for research positions, scholarships, and more. In your application, feel free to mention Prof. Shakeri in your personal statement, emphasizing the relevance of our expertise to your research objectives and future ambitions.

**PostDoc application**
For those seeking a senior research position, please include your CV along with a 1-page statement of your research in your email. Should we decide to proceed, you will be contacted for further discussions.
